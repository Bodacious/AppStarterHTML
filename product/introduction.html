Your product

So you have an idea for an app? Terrific!

First of all, you need to structure and organise your thoughts so you can give a good, detailed, description of the app to other people for their assessment.

You should start this process by putting your thoughts in writing and telling the reader:

  • What the app is all about and a quick overview of what it will do
  • What the key features are, and why they are so great
  • What the main benefits are, and why they are so important to the user
  • What the screens will look like
