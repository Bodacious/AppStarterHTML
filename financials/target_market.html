Target Market

First we need to establish the realistic size of you market.

Let's start by taking the Market Size figure we previously calculated (from Intended Audience on page 8) and try to determine how many of them actually use smartphones. For example, Star Trek fans are likely to keep up with new technology and the percentage may be high, while accountants may have gone down the traditional business route and all have Blackberrys.

Refine your initial Market Size figure down to what you now think is a realistic number. This is called your Target Market.

Your Target Market is the absolute maximum number of sales you would make if everyone you target bought the app.
