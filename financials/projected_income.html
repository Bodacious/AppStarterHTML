Projected Income

So how much can you realistically expect to make from this idea? This is the all important question!

Multiply your Maximum Revenue by your Uptake Percentage to get your best guess income projection. This is your Projected Income figure.

It is reasonable to assume that without any changes to the app, everyone who is likely to buy it will have done so within the first year.
