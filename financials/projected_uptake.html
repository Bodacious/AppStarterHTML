Projected Uptake

Now you need to decide to what is a more realistic income projection. What percentage of your Target Market do you think will actually buy the app? Remember, even if you build a great app, you can't expect everybody to buy it.

If you have no real idea about this, then 1% is a good starting place. This means that one in every hundred will buy the app.

That might seem like a small figure but there are other aspects to consider too, such as: marketing (how many people in your Target Market will get to hear about your product) and competition (how many people will choose to buy a competing product before, or instead of buying yours).

This number is called your Uptake Percentage.

Your task: Calculate your Uptake Percentage
